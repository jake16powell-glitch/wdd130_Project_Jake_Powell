The Story Behind The Box

I spent the better part of my twenties realizing that "being an adult" is mostly just a series of high-stakes chores I never actually signed up for. After one too many 2:00 AM Google searches on how to properly defrost a freezer—and a truly embarrassing amount of time spent looking for a single envelope—I realized that the problem wasn't me; it was the lack of a manual. I created Adult-O-Matic because I believe your mental energy is better spent on your passions, your career, and your actual life, rather than wondering when you last changed your air filter. I’m not here to be your life coach; I’m just here to make sure you have the right tools to pretend you have it all together, one subscription box at a time.
